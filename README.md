# Vasudev Studio | The Visual Asset Engine

![Vasudev Studio Banner](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1618005182384-a83a8bd57fbe?auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=2000&h=600)

## The Philosophy
Vasudev Studio isn't just another gradient generator. It is a high-performance visual laboratory built for designers who demand precision and soul in their digital assets. We created this to bridge the gap between complex design software and the speed of the web, allowing you to manipulate light, color, and texture in real-time.

## The Experience
At its core, the Studio utilizes a custom-built **Chromatic Engine** that simulates fluid mesh gradients. Every point is dynamic, every color is vibrant, and every export is production-ready.

### 🎨 Chromatic Mastery
The sidebar gives you absolute control over the visual spectrum. You can manually tweak individual color points, shuffle palettes for instant inspiration, or scale the complexity by adding up to 10 independent light sources.

### 📐 Precision Composition
*   **Multi-Format Canvas**: Instantly switch between **Desktop (16:9)**, **Social (1:1)**, and **Story (9:16)** ratios.
*   **Textural Grain**: Add a layer of analog warmth with our adjustable grain engine, moving your designs from "flat digital" to "premium physical."
*   **Geometric Overlays**: Toggle dot-matrix or architectural grids to add structure to your fluid compositions.

### ⚡ Professional Workflow
*   **One-Click CSS**: Get the exact radial-gradient code for your web projects instantly.
*   **4K PNG Export**: High-fidelity snapshots at 4x scale for print or digital high-DPI displays.
*   **The Lab History**: A built-in local history system that lets you "Save Lab" states and revisit your favorite experiments anytime.

## The Architecture
Built with performance and modern standards in mind:
- **Core Engine**: Vanilla JavaScript & Tailwind CSS for zero-lag interaction.
- **Rendering**: Real-time DOM manipulation with hardware-accelerated CSS filters.
- **Export Engine**: `dom-to-image-more` for high-resolution asset generation.
